Sunday Message: Fire Revival

2026-04-14

Today we declare revival fire in our lives.

The power of God is moving in this generation. God is calling His people back to prayer, holiness, worship, and total surrender. Revival does not begin outside; it begins in the heart of a believer who is hungry for God.

In this season, we must remain connected, remain in prayer, and remain faithful. The Lord is raising men and women who will carry His fire and spread His light in this dark generation.

Stay connected and remain in prayer.
