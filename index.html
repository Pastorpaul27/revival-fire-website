About Us

Revival Fire Movement is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to awakening hearts, raising believers on fire for God, and spreading the message of Jesus Christ through worship gatherings, prayer meetings, revival campaigns, teaching, outreach, and discipleship.


Our desire is to see lives transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit and to build a generation that walks in holiness, purpose, love, and boldness for Christ.
